1. Purpose ASA 5.3.1, 5.3.2

To ensure equipment used by the service is fit for purpose, ARTG-listed where applicable, and verified before clinical use.

2. Selection

  1. Clinical/technical specification approved by Tech Lead.
  2. ARTG listing confirmed for medical devices (TGA).
  3. Compatibility with existing PSG software and data formats validated.
  4. Total cost of ownership and serviceability assessed.

3. Acceptance Test

On installation (and for loaned equipment), the following are performed and recorded on FRM-005:

  1. Visual / mechanical inspection.
  2. Electrical safety check by qualified biomedical engineer (Class B body-protected supply per cl. 5.2.1).
  3. Full functional test against manufacturer specification.
  4. Signal quality test against known input (square-wave calibration for EEG/EOG/EMG gain, filter and response).
  5. Quantitative-channel verification: SpO2, sound level, PAP pressure, TcCO2, position.
  6. Qualitative-channel verification: nasal pressure, thermal, RIP, microphone, video.
  7. Verification of software version and configuration.
  8. Tech Lead authorisation for clinical use.

4. Records

Acceptance certificate; FRM-005 baseline; entry in equipment register (FRM-004); manuals filed.

5. References

ASA Standard cl. 5.3.1 - 5.3.3, 5.2.1.
