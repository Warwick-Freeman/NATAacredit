DME Supplier 24-Hour Availability Policy

Standard: AASM D-1

1. Purpose

To ensure the DME Supplier maintains 24/7 telephone accessibility for patients and caregivers, per AASM Standard D-1.

2. Policy

The DME Supplier must maintain a designated contact person accessible to patients and/or caregivers via telephone 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 52 weeks per year. All patient education materials and advertising must display the DME Supplier contact information.

3. Procedure

24/7 Contact

  1. A designated contact person is accessible by telephone 24/7/365.
  2. The contact number is prominently displayed in all patient materials and advertising.
  3. An up-to-date on-call schedule is maintained; on-call staff have access to patient records and equipment information.

Patient Communication Materials

  1. All patient education materials and advertising include the DME Supplier contact information.
  2. Contact information is verified for currency regularly.
  3. Patients are informed of 24/7 availability at equipment delivery.

On-Call Responsibilities

  1. On-call staff are trained to address routine questions, troubleshooting, and emergencies.
  2. All after-hours patient contacts are documented in the patient record.
  3. Escalation procedures are defined for situations requiring physician or emergency-service involvement.
