Standard: AASM C-3
⚠ CATEGORY I STANDARD — Non-compliance is immediate cause for denial or revocation of accreditation.

1. Purpose

To ensure that only licensed medical providers diagnose medical conditions, in accordance with AASM Standard C-3 (Category I).

2. Policy

Only a licensed medical provider may diagnose a medical condition. Technical and administrative staff must not communicate diagnoses. Compliance is a Category I requirement.

3. Procedure

Diagnosis Authority

  1. Only a licensed medical provider (physician, DO, or appropriately licensed APP acting within scope and state law) may diagnose a medical condition.
  2. Sleep study interpretation alone does not constitute a diagnosis; diagnosis requires attribution of findings, together with the patient's signs and symptoms, to an underlying sleep disorder by a licensed provider.
  3. Technical and administrative staff must not communicate diagnoses to patients or referring providers.

Documentation

  1. All diagnoses are documented in the patient medical record by the licensed provider.
  2. Reports to referring providers identify the interpreting/diagnosing physician or licensed provider.
  3. The Site Director ensures all staff understand the limits of their scope with respect to diagnosis.

Telemedicine

  1. Telemedicine tools may be used in accordance with applicable state laws and AASM recommendations.
  2. All telemedicine diagnoses are documented per applicable state and federal regulations.
