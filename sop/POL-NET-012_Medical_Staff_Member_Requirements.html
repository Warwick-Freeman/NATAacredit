⚠ CATEGORY I STANDARD — Non-compliance is immediate cause for denial or revocation of accreditation.

1. Purpose

To ensure all medical staff members meet the qualifications required by the AASM Standards for Accreditation and hold valid licensure appropriate to their practice.

2. Policy

All medical staff members, including physicians, licensed psychologists, and advanced practice providers (NP, PA, APRN), must hold a valid, unrestricted state licence in states where patients are evaluated, diagnosed, or treated. The AASM recommends board-certification in sleep medicine. Medical staff members must maintain required CME. Compliance with N-6 is a Category I requirement.

3. Procedure

Qualifications and Licensure (N-6 — Category I)

  1. Medical staff members include physicians, licensed psychologists, and advanced practice providers (NP, PA, APRN).
  2. All medical staff members must hold a valid, unrestricted state licence in each state where patients are evaluated, diagnosed, or treated.
  3. Licensure is verified upon hire and re-verified annually; copies are maintained in personnel files.
  4. Medical staff members are strongly encouraged to obtain board certification in sleep medicine.

Continuing Medical Education (N-7)

  1. Medical staff members must earn at least 30 AMA PRA Category 1 CME credits in sleep medicine, averaged at 10 per year over the past 36 months.
  2. CME compliance must be documented and maintained on file.
  3. The CME requirement is waived for 36 months from completion of a sleep medicine fellowship or initial board certification.
  4. Staff who completed formal training within the previous 12 months have their credits prorated from the program end date (or hire date for APPs).
  5. Entity-run education sessions are acceptable if they have defined objectives and a signed attendance roster.
