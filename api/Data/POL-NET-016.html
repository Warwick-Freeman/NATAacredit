⚠ CATEGORY I STANDARD — Non-compliance is immediate cause for denial or revocation of accreditation.

1. Purpose

To ensure all entity physician staff follow the current AMA Code of Medical Ethics, in accordance with AASM Standard N-16 (Category I).

2. Policy

All entity staff and physician staff members are required to follow the current opinions in the Code of Medical Ethics of the American Medical Association (AMA) Council on Ethical and Judicial Affairs. The entity must maintain readily accessible access to the Code. Compliance is a Category I requirement.

3. Procedure

Access to the AMA Code of Medical Ethics

  1. The current AMA Code of Medical Ethics must be readily accessible. It is available at: https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/code-medical-ethics-overview
  2. All physicians and relevant staff are informed of how to access the Code.
  3. The entity reviews the Code annually for updates.

Staff Compliance

  1. All physician staff members attest annually that they have reviewed and will abide by the current AMA Code of Medical Ethics.
  2. The Site Director is responsible for assuring staff compliance, including compliance with any institutional ethics requirements.
  3. Annual attestation documentation is maintained in personnel files.

Reporting Ethical Concerns

  1. Staff members are encouraged to report ethical concerns to the Site Director or Network Director.
  2. Reported concerns are reviewed and addressed promptly.
  3. Documentation of reported concerns and their resolution is maintained.
