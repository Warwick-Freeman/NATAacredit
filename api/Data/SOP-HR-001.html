1. Purpose ASA 5.1

To ensure all staff are appropriately qualified, trained and competent for their tasks, and to maintain ongoing competence.

ℹ️ Nexus 360 Integration: Steps in this procedure that are performed or supported by the Nexus 360 quality management system are identified with [Nexus 360] throughout this document. Staff must access Nexus 360 at http://localhost:5173 to complete these steps. The system audit trail satisfies record-keeping requirements for all digitally recorded activities.

2. Roles and Minimum Qualifications

3. Induction

All new staff complete a documented orientation including QMS, safety, infection control, BLS, fire and emergency procedures, IT and confidentiality.

4. BLS Competence

All clinical and technical staff hold current BLS, recertified annually. Paediatric BLS required for paediatric labs.

5. Training Records

Staff competency records, training history, and proficiency assessments are maintained in Nexus 360 Staff & Training module [Nexus 360]; training due dates and gaps are tracked automatically. FRM-014 may supplement for offline recording.

6. Competency Assessment

  1. Initial competency demonstrated against established criteria for each task before independent performance.
  2. Ongoing competency assessed periodically (target: annually).
  3. Assessment recorded in Nexus 360 Staff & Training module [Nexus 360] (FRM-014 supplementary for offline use).

7. Continuing Education

Each staff member has an annual development plan. Senior staff supported to attend relevant professional meetings. CPD logged.

8. Appraisal

Annual appraisal with written report; staff made aware of report contents; plan to address deficiencies documented.

9. References

ASA Standard cl. 5.1.1 - 5.1.3.
