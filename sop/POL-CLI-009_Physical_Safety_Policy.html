1. Purpose

To ensure the Sleep Clinic physical space complies with all applicable construction, fire-safety, and building codes.

2. Policy

The physical space used for the Sleep Clinic must comply with all required standards, regulations, and codes for construction, fire safety, and building codes applicable in the jurisdiction where services are provided.

3. Procedure

Regulatory Compliance

  1. Facility complies with all applicable construction codes, fire-safety regulations, and building codes.
  2. Evidence of compliance (e.g., Certificate of Occupancy, fire inspection certificates) is maintained on file.
  3. Identified deficiencies are documented and corrected promptly.

Annual Review

  1. Site Director conducts an annual physical safety compliance review.
  2. Changes to regulations or codes affecting the location are reviewed and implemented.

Fire Safety

  1. Functioning fire detection and suppression systems are in place as required by local codes.
  2. Fire evacuation routes are posted in visible locations throughout the facility.
