1. Purpose ASA 4.3

To ensure all documents that form the management system are uniquely identified, approved before issue, current at point of use, and removed or marked when obsolete.

ℹ️ Nexus 360 Integration: Steps in this procedure that are performed or supported by the Nexus 360 quality management system are identified with [Nexus 360] throughout this document. Staff must access Nexus 360 at http://localhost:5173 to complete these steps. The system audit trail satisfies record-keeping requirements for all digitally recorded activities.

2. Scope

Applies to internally generated and externally sourced documents including policies, SOPs, forms, equipment manuals, software, templates, calibration records, informational documents and educational guides, in any media (paper or electronic).

3. Responsibilities

  • Quality Manager - maintains the master controlled-document register; approves issue and obsolescence; coordinates periodic review.
  • Document Owner - drafts and revises; ensures content accuracy; nominated reviewer.
  • Authoriser (Medical Director / Tech Lead) - signs documents for issue.
  • All staff - use only current authorised versions.

4. Document Identification

Every controlled document carries: title; document ID (prefix-area-nnn); revision number; effective date; next review date; authoriser; page x of y.

5. Procedure

5.1 Drafting

Document Owner drafts using the approved HTML template; initiates a Document Change Request via the Nexus 360 document workflow [Nexus 360] (FRM-027 is used only when Nexus 360 is unavailable).

5.2 Review

Reviewer checks content, ASA-clause alignment and consistency with related documents; reviewer actions are tracked in the Nexus 360 document workflow [Nexus 360]; comments returned to Owner.

5.3 Approval & Issue

Authoriser signs; Quality Manager assigns the revision number, updates the master register in Nexus 360 [Nexus 360], distributes the controlled copy and withdraws the prior version.

5.4 Periodic Review

Default review cycle 24 months. Nexus 360 generates review-due alerts automatically [Nexus 360] to owners 60 days before due date. Even where no change is required, the review is documented.

5.5 Hand Amendment

Where a document is amended by hand pending re-issue, the change is initialled and dated by the Authoriser; the amended copy is treated as current and a re-issue is scheduled within 30 days.

5.6 Obsolescence

Withdrawn documents are removed from points of use. Copies retained for legal or knowledge purposes are watermarked "OBSOLETE - REFERENCE ONLY".

5.7 Electronic Documents

Nexus 360 is the authoritative QMS repository [Nexus 360]; changes to electronic templates and computerised forms follow the same workflow with version control; access permissions restrict editing to Document Owners. Staff access only current approved versions via Nexus 360.

6. Records

Master controlled-document register maintained in Nexus 360 Documents & SOPs module [Nexus 360]; Document Change Request workflow records in Nexus 360; FRM-027 used only when system unavailable; periodic review records; distribution log.

7. References

ASA Standard cl. 4.3.1 - 4.3.3.2.
