1. Purpose:

To ensure that HSAT staff receive proper training in using the HSAT device(s) in accordance with all applicable policies and procedures.

2. Policy

All HSAT staff who participate in the HSAT program will be trained in the appropriate use of the HSAT device. The training will be conducted by either the site director, other appropriately trained staff members, or a representative from the manufacturer. All training will be documented, and a copy will be maintained in the staff’s employee file.

(insert name) __________will be responsible for training patients on the use of the device selected to be used. Documentation will be maintained in the employee’s the Nexus 360 Staff & Training module.

operations and optimal use of the device

proper application of sensors

troubleshooting technical issues

specific safety protocols and warnings pertinent to the device
