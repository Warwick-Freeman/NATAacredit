1. Purpose ASA 4.3.2.1

The Master Document Register is the single controlled list of all current SOPs, policies and forms maintained under the QMS. It identifies for each document the owner, authoriser, mapped ASA clause(s), linked SOP/Form, current revision and next review date. The definitive controlled-document register is maintained in Nexus 360 Documents & SOPs module [Nexus 360]; this printed register is a reference copy.

ℹ️ Nexus 360 Integration: Steps in this procedure that are performed or supported by the Nexus 360 quality management system are identified with [Nexus 360] throughout this document. Staff must access Nexus 360 at http://localhost:5173 to complete these steps. The system audit trail satisfies record-keeping requirements for all digitally recorded activities.

2. Maintenance

Maintained by the Quality Manager. Updated whenever a document is issued, revised or made obsolete (per SOP-QMS-002). Reviewed at every management review.
