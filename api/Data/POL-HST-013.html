Standard: AASM H-7

1. Purpose

To ensure all HSAT reports include required parameters and that management recommendations are consistent with AASM guidelines.

2. Policy

All HSAT reports must include all RECOMMENDED and/or ACCEPTED parameters from the HSAT Rules for Adults chapter of the current AASM Scoring Manual. Any management recommendations must be consistent with applicable AASM practice guidelines.

3. Procedure

Report Requirements

  1. HSAT reports include all RECOMMENDED/ACCEPTED parameters from the current AASM Scoring Manual HSAT chapter.
  2. Reports identify the interpreting board-certified sleep medicine provider.
  3. Where the Scoring Manual allows scoring options, the report states which option was used.
  4. Reports are completed and filed in the patient record in a timely manner.

Recommendations

  1. Management recommendations are consistent with applicable AASM practice guidelines.
  2. Recommendations are based on test results and available clinical information.
  3. Recommendations must not include a medical diagnosis; diagnosis is made by a licensed provider (Standard C-3).

Review and Sign-Off

  1. All HSAT reports are reviewed and signed by an individual board-certified in sleep medicine (Standards N-1, N-3) as required by Standard H-2 (Category I).
  2. Finalised reports are returned to the ordering provider in a timely manner.
  3. A copy of all finalised reports is retained in the patient record.
