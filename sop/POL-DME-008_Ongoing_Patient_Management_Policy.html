patient management/On-going Follow-up

1. Purpose

Ongoing follow-up will be provided to ensure patient compliance and safety with the type of medical equipment used and that it is consistent with the physician’s order.

2. Policy

It is the policy to regularly assess and monitor patients using medical equipment and communicate any concerns or issues promptly to the ordering health care professional.

3. Procedure

The durable medical equipment (DME) supplier will schedule regular follow-up appointments with the patient to monitor equipment usage and compliance and address any concerns or issues.

Each patient who is set up on (insert type of equipment) will be contacted by a respiratory therapist/sleep technologist/sleep technician within (insert # of days) days of setup.

Assess patient’s ability to acclimate to its use and answer any questions or concerns, provide further education and encouragement on its use.

This contact may be a phone call, if preferred by the patient, or in person.

Compliance data is obtained, if applicable, via modem and SD card monitoring.

Any issues identified because of the follow-up will be communicated in writing to the physician, such as:

patient compliance and usage from PAP device

challenges the patient may face with therapy

device setting adjustment is needed based on clinical assessment and patient feedback

All follow-up assessments, interventions and encounters will be documented in the patient record.
