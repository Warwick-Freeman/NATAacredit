1. Purpose ASA 4.7

To establish two-way communication with referrers and patients and to systematically capture and act on their feedback.

2. Feedback Channels

  • FRM-012 Patient Feedback Survey - issued post study and at therapy follow-up; quarterly aggregate analysis.
  • FRM-013 Referrer Feedback Survey - issued annually and after every report dispute.
  • Verbal and email feedback recorded in feedback register.
  • Suggestion box (electronic and physical) for staff and patients.

3. Analysis & Action

Quality Manager reviews feedback monthly; themes converted to CAPA, suggestions or QMS changes; positive feedback shared with staff. All material feedback reported at management review.

4. Records

Feedback register; survey results; CAPA links.

5. References

ASA Standard cl. 4.7.1.
