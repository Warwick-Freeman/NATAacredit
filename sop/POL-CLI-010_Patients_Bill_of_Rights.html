1. Purpose

To ensure all patients are informed of their rights when receiving Sleep Clinic services.

2. Policy

All patients are informed of their rights, which are posted in a visible location and provided to each patient at or before the time of service.

3. Procedure

Patient Rights

  1. The right to accurate and easily understood information about their health care and providers; assistance is provided if the patient has language or disability barriers.
  2. The right to know treatment options and participate in care decisions; representatives may act on behalf of patients lacking decision-making capacity.
  3. The right to considerate, respectful care that does not discriminate.
  4. The right to privacy of communications with providers and protection of health information under HIPAA.
  5. The right to inspect, copy, and request amendment of their medical records.
  6. The right to a detailed explanation of any bill and information about available financial assistance.

Notification

  1. Patients' Bill of Rights is available to all patients and posted in a clearly visible location.
  2. Staff explain patients' rights upon request.
  3. Rights document is reviewed annually and updated as needed.

Complaints

  1. Patients may file a complaint without fear of retaliation.
  2. Contact information for filing complaints, including with relevant regulatory bodies, is provided to patients.
