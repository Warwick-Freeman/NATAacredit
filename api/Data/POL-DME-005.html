  1. EQUIPMENT LOANER POLICY

1. Purpose

To establish guidelines for the provision of loaner medical equipment to patients whose medical equipment is in need of repair or replacement due to poor performance or malfunction.

2. Policy

It is the policy to provide comparable loaner equipment to patients in need of repair or replacement through the duration of the repair timeframe. Loaner equipment will be provided at no charge. Patients who damage or lose loaner equipment are susceptible to repair or replacement fees for the product.

3. Procedure

Patients may request an equipment loan based upon medical necessity.

Tracking of the equipment will be recorded in the Nexus 360 , which will include:

serial number(s) of both the equipment/item returned and loaned

date the equipment and/or items were brought in for repair

date the equipment and/or items were lent out.

to whom the equipment and/or item was lent (name and address)

the date the equipment or item was returned

Patients are responsible for the proper use and care of the loaned equipment.

A written agreement outlining the terms and conditions for the loan will be signed by the patient and _______(insert supplier representative)__________.

Any damage or loss is to be reported immediately.

Patients who damage or lose equipment may be susceptible to repair and/or replacement fees.
