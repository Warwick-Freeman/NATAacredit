Standard: AASM S-7

1. Purpose

To ensure the DME Supplier service location has appropriate emergency equipment, properly maintained and staff trained in its use.

2. Policy

The DME service location must have immediate access to either an AED on-site or an on-site medical emergency response team. All emergency equipment must be maintained per manufacturer recommendations.

3. Procedure

Required Equipment

  1. Immediate access to AED on-site, or an on-site medical emergency response team.
  2. Equipment location clearly communicated to all staff.

Maintenance

  1. Emergency equipment maintained and documented per manufacturer recommendations.
  2. AED battery and pad expiration checked monthly and documented.
  3. Deficiencies corrected immediately.

Staff Training

  1. All patient-contact staff maintain current BLS/CPR certification including manikin skills training.
  2. Training documented; refresher training provided annually.
