Standard: AASM S-7

1. Purpose

To ensure the Sleep Clinic has appropriate emergency equipment, properly maintained and staff trained in its use.

2. Policy

The Sleep Clinic must have immediate access to either an AED on-site or an on-site medical emergency response team. All emergency equipment must be maintained per manufacturer recommendations and staff trained in its use.

3. Procedure

Required Equipment

  1. Immediate access to AED on-site, or an on-site medical emergency response team.
  2. AED location is clearly communicated to all staff.
  3. Emergency equipment is accessible at all times when services are provided.

Equipment Maintenance

  1. Emergency equipment maintained and documented per manufacturer recommendations.
  2. AED battery and pad expiration checked and documented at least monthly.
  3. Deficiencies corrected immediately and documented.

Staff Training

  1. All patient-care staff maintain current BLS/CPR certification including manikin skills training.
  2. Training on all emergency equipment documented for each staff member in the training section of Nexus 360.
  3. New staff trained before working independently with patients; refresher training annually.
