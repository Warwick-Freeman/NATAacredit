Most bomb threats are received by phone. Always treat any bomb threat as serious and real until proven otherwise. Act quickly; obtain as much information using the checklist, if possible.

Remain calm; keep the caller on the phone as long as possible and DO NOT HANG UP even if the caller does. Listen carefully, be polite, show interest and learn as much information as possible. If able, communicate the situation to another individual who can contact police emergency services. Record the caller’s phone number.
