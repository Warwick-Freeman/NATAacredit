1. Purpose ASA 5.8.12

To ensure reports are transmitted securely and only to authorised recipients.

2. Verbal Release

Verbal release permitted only by a reporting physician to the referring doctor or delegate; recipient identity confirmed; content limited to confirmed result; entry made in patient file (date, time, recipient, content).

3. Electronic Transmission

  • Secure messaging (Argus, HealthLink, FHIR) preferred.
  • Encrypted email where secure messaging not available; recipient confirmed; encryption keys managed.
  • Fax permitted to confirmed numbers with cover sheet.

4. Web Portal

Portal access requires authenticated login; reports downloadable only in protected (non-editable) format. Access logs retained.

5. Authorisation

Reports issued only when appropriately authorised; off-site issue (eg remote reporting) covered by documented arrangements reviewed at NATA assessment.

6. Retention

Copies of issued reports retained by or accessible to the accredited service; care taken to retain copies of any handwritten comments at the issuing site.

7. References

ASA Standard cl. 5.8.12.
