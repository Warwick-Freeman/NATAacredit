Standard: AASM C-6

1. Purpose

To ensure all professional staff who prescribe controlled substances maintain valid DEA licences and comply with applicable federal and state regulations.

2. Policy

All professional staff (MDs, DOs, PhDs, and APPs) who administer, dispense, or prescribe controlled substances must maintain valid, unrestricted DEA licences and adhere to all applicable federal and state controlled substance regulations.

3. Procedure

DEA Licensing

  1. All staff who prescribe, dispense, or administer controlled substances must hold a valid DEA registration in each applicable state.
  2. DEA registrations are renewed before expiration; copies are maintained in personnel files.
  3. The Site Director verifies DEA registration upon hire and annually.

State Requirements

  1. Staff comply with all state-specific PDMP requirements and prescribing rules.
  2. Changes to state or federal controlled substance regulations are reviewed and implemented promptly.

Documentation

  1. All controlled substance prescriptions are documented in the patient's medical record with clear indication.
  2. Prescribing records are maintained per DEA and applicable state record-keeping requirements.
  3. Concerns regarding diversion or misuse are reported to the Site Director immediately.
