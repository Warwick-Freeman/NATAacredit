1. Purpose ASA 4.6

To define how purchased external services, equipment and consumable supplies that affect the quality of the service are selected, evaluated and used.

2. Selection Criteria

  • Technical specifications relevant to clinical purpose.
  • ARTG listing for therapeutic goods (cl. 5.2.1).
  • Vendor quality and after-sales support.
  • Total cost of ownership (capital + consumables + service).
  • Compatibility with existing systems and data formats.

3. Procedure

  1. Need identified by Tech Lead; specification documented.
  2. At least two quotes obtained where practicable.
  3. Supplier evaluation against criteria; Medical Director or delegate approves purchase.
  4. On receipt, equipment acceptance test per SOP-EQ-001 before use.
  5. Supplier added to the approved supplier register.
  6. Supplier performance reviewed annually (on-time delivery, quality, responsiveness) and at management review.

4. Records

Approved supplier register; quotes; purchase orders; acceptance test records; supplier performance reviews.

5. References
